Instructions & Fee Schedule

Enter the day, time, and vehicle type to calculate the tunnel fee. The fee depends on whether your journey occurs during peak or off-peak periods.

No Charges: 22:00 - 06:00 and on Christmas Day

Northbound: 06:00 - 10:00 (Peak), all other times off-peak
Southbound: 16:00 - 19:00 (Peak), all other times off-peak

Both directions: Off-peak only (06:00 - 22:00)
